How to play the game.

  • Battle Royale

    Teams fight in an ever shrinking map to be the
    last squad standing. All 60 players start in the main
    ship and can choose to land where they please, finding
    weapons, ammo, attachments and armour in care packages,
    supply bins and on the ground.
    Every few minutes the ring closes and the map gets tighter,
    forcing players to come out of hiding. Truly a game where
    anything can happen.

    This mode can be played in either:

    1. Solo

    2. Duos

    3. Trios

    Although many pros choose to go at it solo, an average team
    with passable communication and teamwork often make short work
    of even the most nimble and accurate predators, making Battle
    Royale - Trios the most popular game mode.

  • Arenas

    A new mode added only 4 months ago and is quickly rising in popularity
    Arenas is Battle Royale refined.
    Without the chaos of landing somewhere and potentially having
    to fight off 4 squads (all trying to punch you to death), Arenas
    pits only two teams against each other, but in a much smaller map
    Both teams start in a safe spawn with limited points to buy some
    basic weapons before. The main difference is that downed players
    can not be brought back (like in Battle Royale). There are also
    material canisters on each map that can be collected to grant extra
    points for buying gear for the following rounds.
    Speaking of, rounds are played until one team wins with a two game
    lead or a stalemate is reached at 8 rounds and sudden death begins.
    A 'winner takes all' round where players get max armour and enough
    points to buy just about anything they want.

  • Ranked

    Pretty much each of teh above, but withe a permanent score after each game.
    Each match has an entry cost of ranked points (RP), with more being earned
    through kills, surviving longer and thus placing in the top 10, then top 5
    and finally the top 3. Winning a match earns far more points than losing, but
    losing is sometimes just as profitable if you played well and killed the
    champion (each match has one, usually the top player from the preious match.)

    Ranking up consitently will move you through the ranks:

    1. Bronze
    2. Silver
    3. Gold
    4. Platinum
    5. Diamond
    6. Masters
    7. Predators

    Apex Predator is the best of the best (if the name didn't give it away).
    There are only ever 100 people allowed in that rank, and those players are
    globally ranked.
