The Legends of Apex


Legend Passive Tactical Ultimate
Bloodhound Foes leave behind clues for you to find. Briefly reveal enemies, traps, and clues throughout all structures in front of you. Enhances your senses, allowing you to see cold tracks and move faster.
Gibraltar Aiming grants an arm shield. Dome blocks incoming and outgoing attacks. Call in a concentrated mortar strike on a position you mark with smoke.
Lifeline Deploy drone to help knocked teammates. Deploy drone that restores health. Call in a drop-pod full of high quality defensive gear.
Pathfinder Scanning beacons reduces zipline wait. Arm grapple-hook up to 50m. Launches a zipline for the team.
Wraith Hears voices when being aimed at by enemies. Phases and becomes faster and does not take damage. Link 2 locations with portals for 60 seconds.
Bangalore Runs faster when being shot at. Fires a smoke screen, twice. Calls in grid-like bombardment.
Caustic Immune to gas traps and highlights enemies in gas. Deploys up to 6 gas traps at a time. Throws a gas grenade, dealing increasing damage over time.
Mirage Goes invisible when reviving teammates. Sends out a holo-decoy that mimics sound and actions. Goes invisible and sends out 8 decoys at once.
Octane Regenerates 1 health per second. Uses a stim to run faster, at the cost of 18 health. Throws a jump-pad for the team.
Watson Regenerates 1 shield every 2 seconds, and ultimate accelerants charge her ult fully. Places electrified fences that damage and slow enemies. Places an arc-pylon that charges shields and destroys grenades.
Crypto Teammamtes can see enemies highlighted by drone within 30m. Deploys remote control drone. Drone fires an emp that does 50 shield damage.
Revenant Moves faster while crouched and climbs much higher. Fires an are of effect grenade that stops enemies from using their abilities. Deploys a totem that has limited range and no shields, but restores half health when shot.
Loba Can see rare and golden loot through walls and care packages. Throws bracelet in an arc and allows her to teleport. Places down a staff that allows players to take any loot within radius.
Rampart Increased magazine capacity and reload speeds with lmg's and minigun. Deploys one-way barriers that block, and deal extra damage when fired through. Deploys a minigun.
Horizon Increase air control and reduce fall impacts. Gravity lift boosts players high into the air. Throws out a black hole that damages and slows enemies.
Fuse Can carry double the amount of grenades, and launch them further. Shoots a knuckle cluster grenade. Shoots a ring of fire that damages and highlights enemies insdie.
Valkyrie Has vtol jets that allow her to fly/ hover for a while, and scans enemies while repositioning. Fires a swarm of rockets that slow and damage enemies. Launches into the sky, with teammates, and can reposition.
Seer Has a heartbeat sensor that shows enemies locations up to 75m. Fires a scan that damages enemies and tracks them for 8 seconds. Deploys a drone-dome that scans enemies moving or shooting within.
